Spoonful Overnight Oats

Empowering uniqueness through an accessible well balance meal.

Spoonful started as a response to address the true impact poor diet has on our lives and how it becomes the root of our physical and emotional struggles.

Spoonful.life

The Challenge

This project was designed together with Camila Candia. As for any new brand there was a temptation to try to fit into the visual codes imposed by the category's referents, losing the possibility of communicating from its own original history. Spoonful's differential was in its connection with ordinary people, with their day-to-day life, without promises of perfection that rarely come true. The great challenge was to build a brand and a product that would provide people with the necessary tools to meet their nutritional goals in a sustainable way.

The Solution

To change the paradigm. Today we are not trying to follow a diet plan, we are trying to pursue a well-balanced nutrition that promotes our general well-being. We builded a brand strategy focused on the latter, aiming to democratize access to convenient and tasty meals, Spoonful will do this through an inclusive, empathetic and realistic way of communicate its benefits. The visual identity will be bright, vibrant and expressive and it will show that different is good and uniqueness is a value for all of us.
